Concepts that are taught symbolically but are really dynamic or spatial. Each playground is a single page you can poke at — drag a probe, turn a dial, add a term — with a challenge to clear. Everything runs locally in your browser: no accounts, no network calls, and your progress is saved on this device.

Built on one shared engine — a 2-D grapher, a 3-D surface renderer, a contour map, a vector-field view, and a common control and scoring shell. Adding a concept means writing its maths, not its machinery.
